About Us

Welcome to Guide English Boarding School

Nestled in the heart of Kamalamai, Sindhuli, we are dedicated to delivering holistic education and nurturing young minds in a safe, caring environment. Our approach blends academic excellence with character building.

From Nursery to Class 10, every child is guided by passionate educators who believe in unleashing each student's full potential through creativity, discipline, and love of learning.
